How to use this app
by Ilmee Mintz
What is this?
This is your daily habit tracker for the 100-Day Weight Loss Challenge. It is built around one idea: consistency, not perfection. You do not need a perfect day. You need to show up.
The method
This system is based on portion control — not calorie counting, not food elimination, not extreme dieting. You eat real food in the right amounts and you track it every day for 100 days. That repetition is what builds the habit that lasts.
What one serving looks like
🥦
Vegetables — 1 cup per box. Aim for 5 cups a day. Raw, cooked, any kind.
🥩
Protein — 2/3 cup per box. Meat, fish, eggs, legumes, tofu. Choose 3 servings for most days, or 4 if you are a hungrier person.
🍚
Carbs — 1/2 cup per box. Rice, oats, bread, potato. Optional. Unchecked carb boxes do not affect your streak.
🍎
Fruit — 1 cup per box. Fresh or frozen. Aim for 3 servings.
💧
Water — 500 ml per box. 6 boxes = done. 7 and 8 are bonus.
👟
Steps — 3 boxes = 3000 steps minimum. 5 boxes = 5000 steps = done. More is bonus.
🌙
Sleep — 6 or more hours. Check the box if you slept enough.
💪
Exercise — 10 min per box. 2 boxes = 20 minutes = done. 3rd box is bonus.
How to keep your streak
Complete at least 5 out of 7 required categories each day. Carbs do not count toward the streak. Lock your day before midnight using the Lock My Day button.
The most important rule
Start again. Every single day. Miss a day — start again. Break your streak — start again. The goal is not a perfect 100 days. The goal is to keep coming back.
